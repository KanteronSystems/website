About us

Kanteron Systems was a privately held Healthcare IT Computational Bioinformatics company founded in 2005 and closed in 2021 that helped healthcare providers manage imaging and genomic data effectively for precision medicine applications at the point of care.


With offices in Madrid (Spain), and HQ in Valencia (Spain), Kanteron Systems' solution was deployed over 500 times in 15 countries, at some of the world's largest healthcare networks like the NHS in the UK, ISSSTE and IMSS in Mexico, or EsSalud in Peru.
